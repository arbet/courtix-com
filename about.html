About Courtix Hosting

Building reliable software for businesses that need results, not excuses.

Who We Are

Courtix Hosting is a software development company based in Dover, Delaware, United States. We specialize in building custom software, web applications, and mobile apps for businesses of all sizes — from startups launching their first product to established companies modernizing their operations.

We started with a simple idea: businesses deserve development partners who understand both the technical and business sides of a project. Too many companies end up with software that technically works but doesn't solve the actual problem. We bridge that gap.

Our team brings together experienced developers, designers, and project managers who have delivered projects across industries including fintech, e-commerce, healthcare, logistics, and SaaS.

What We Stand For

Transparency

No hidden fees, no scope surprises. We communicate openly about timelines, costs, and challenges. You always know where your project stands.

Quality Code

We write clean, maintainable, well-tested code. Every project includes documentation so your team can understand and extend the work we deliver.

Long-Term Partnerships

We don't disappear after launch. We build relationships with our clients and provide ongoing support, maintenance, and feature development.

Business-First Thinking

Technology is a means to an end. We focus on your business objectives first and choose the right tools and approaches to achieve them efficiently.
