Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Send us a message using the form above or email us directly at hello@courtix.com. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project, understand your requirements, and provide a quote.

How much does a typical project cost?

Project costs vary based on scope and complexity. Custom software starts from $2,500, web and mobile development from $1,500, and consulting from $500. We provide detailed quotes after the initial consultation. All prices are in US Dollars (USD).

What is your payment process?

We typically require a deposit (30–50% of the project total) to begin work, with the remainder billed at agreed milestones or upon completion. We accept Visa and Mastercard.

What if I need to cancel or request a refund?

Please review our Refund & Cancellation Policy for full details. In general, refunds before work begins are available minus a small administrative fee. After work has started, refunds are calculated pro-rata based on completed work.

Do you work with clients outside the US?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. Our team is experienced in remote collaboration across time zones. All services are delivered digitally.
