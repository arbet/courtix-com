## The challenge

The operator&rsquo;s dispatchers were juggling three separate tools to get a live picture of their fleet. Position data was stale by the time it reached the office, historical route replays took hours to generate, and the driver app was so frustrating to use that most drivers ignored it.

## Our approach

- Designed a single Go backend handling both ingest and the WebSocket fan-out.
- Stored position data in PostgreSQL with PostGIS for geospatial queries.
- Rebuilt the driver companion app in React Native with offline-first sync.
- Added route replay as a first-class feature with sub-second scrubbing.

## Outcome

- **400+ vehicles** streaming position data in real time
- **99.97% uptime** over the first year of operation
- **Route replay** latency down from hours to under one second
- Driver app adoption jumped from 30% to 94% of active drivers
