## The challenge

A professional services firm wanted to let their associates ask questions across a 40,000-document contract library. Off-the-shelf tools either hallucinated citations or couldn&rsquo;t handle the document volume. They also needed a defensible audit trail of every query and answer for partner review.

## Our approach

- Built an ingestion pipeline with chunking, metadata extraction and pgvector embeddings.
- Used a managed LLM API with prompt caching and structured tool use for citation-first answers.
- Enforced strict retrieval gating: every answer must ground in cited, linked source passages.
- Wrapped it in a clean Next.js interface with saved queries, review threads and export to DOCX.

## Outcome

- **70% reduction** in average contract review time
- **Every answer** linked to verifiable source passages (no ungrounded output)
- **40,000+ documents** indexed and searchable in seconds
- Full audit trail of every query, answer and citation for compliance review
