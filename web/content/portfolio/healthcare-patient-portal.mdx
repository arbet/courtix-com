## The challenge

A growing specialty clinic group wanted to offer modern digital services to their patients without tripping over HIPAA requirements or their existing EHR integration contract. The previous vendor&rsquo;s portal was unmaintained, ugly and had no audit trail.

## Our approach

- Architected around AWS primitives: RDS with KMS-managed encryption, WAF in front of CloudFront, signed S3 URLs for documents.
- Implemented append-only audit logging at the database and application level, with tamper-evident checksums.
- Built an access-control layer with role hierarchies matching the clinic&rsquo;s organisation.
- Shipped a clean, accessible Next.js frontend that actually helps patients find what they need.

## Outcome

- **HIPAA audit passed** on first review — zero findings
- **Patient adoption** reached 68% within three months
- **Support calls** about portal issues dropped 80%
- Every access to patient data is auditable and attributable
