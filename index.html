Payment Security & Delivery

Payment Security: All card transactions are processed through Wise, a regulated financial institution. Your card details are encrypted using industry-standard TLS encryption and are never stored on our servers. We comply with PCI DSS requirements to ensure your payment information is handled securely.

Transaction Currency: All prices and transactions are processed in US Dollars (USD, $). Your card issuer may apply their own exchange rate if your card is denominated in a different currency.

Delivery of Services: All our services are delivered digitally. Upon successful payment:

  • Software development projects: Commence within 5 business days of deposit payment. Delivery timelines are agreed upon in the project scope document.
  • Consulting services: Scheduled within 3 business days of booking confirmation.

As all services are delivered digitally, there are no physical shipping arrangements. A confirmation email with order details is sent immediately upon successful payment.

Export Restrictions: Our digital services are available worldwide. However, we are unable to provide services to individuals or entities in countries subject to US sanctions. It is the buyer's responsibility to ensure compliance with local import regulations for digital services.

Country of Origin: Courtix Hosting LLC operates from the United States (Delaware). All services originate from and are governed by US jurisdiction.
